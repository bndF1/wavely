Features

Built for how you
actually listen.

Everything a podcast player needs. Nothing you don't. Designed after Google Podcasts — clean, fast, and focused on content.

🎧

Beautiful Full-Screen Player

Large artwork, smooth scrubber, configurable playback speed from 0.5× to 2×, and 30-second skip controls. Music-app quality for podcasts.

📱

One App, Every Platform

Use Wavely as a native iOS app, native Android app, or install it as a PWA on your desktop. Same experience, everywhere you go.

🌙

System Dark Mode

Automatically follows your system preference. Override manually anytime. Easy on the eyes for late-night listening sessions.

📶

Listen Offline

The app shell and your recent content cache automatically. Keep listening when the network disappears — on the subway, in the sky, anywhere.

🔍

Search All Podcasts

Instant search across millions of podcasts. Browse by category and trending shows. Find anything, subscribe in one tap.

📋

Your Library, Your Way

Subscribe to shows, bookmark episodes, manage a playback queue. Full podcast and episode detail pages with everything you need.

App Preview

A player that stays
out of your way.

Inspired by the simplicity of Google Podcasts. Every screen is focused on the content, not the interface.

01
Full-screen player Artwork-first layout with scrubber, speed controls, and skip. Music-app quality for podcasts.
02
Persistent mini-player Never lose your place. The mini-player docks above the tab bar across every screen.
03
Episode list & detail Full show pages with episode history, descriptions, and one-tap playback.
04
Bottom tab navigation Home, Browse, Search, Library — everything reachable with your thumb.

For Developers

Built in the open.
Contribute freely.

Wavely is a reference implementation of Angular + Ionic + Capacitor — built with every dependency at its latest stable version. Fork it, study it, improve it.

View on GitHub Open an Issue
01
Clone & run locally bun install → bun start — up and running in under a minute.
02
Pick an open issue Browse the GitHub issue tracker — features, bugs, and good first issues.
03
Open a pull request CI runs automatically: lint, type-check, tests, and build on every PR.
