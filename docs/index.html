Everything you need to listen

Powerful features wrapped in a simple, intuitive interface.

🔍

Smart Discovery

Browse top charts by country, search any podcast on iTunes, and explore curated categories. Country-aware trending keeps local charts front and centre.

📻

Internet Radio

Thousands of live radio stations from around the world. Browse by genre, country, or language — and mark your favourites for quick access.

📋

Queue & Up Next

Tap "Play Next" on any episode to jump it to the front of your queue. Enable auto-queue to have your feed line up automatically as you listen.

☁️

Cloud Sync

Sign in with Google to sync subscriptions, listening history, and progress across all your devices. Start on your phone, finish on your laptop.

🎧

Advanced Player

Variable playback speed (0.5×–2×), skip back 15s / forward 30s, and a persistent mini-player that follows you as you browse. On desktop, skip controls appear directly in the mini-player. Your listening history is always a tap away.

📡

Offline Ready

Built as a PWA — install it on any device and use it reliably even on spotty connections. Artwork and app shell are cached for instant loads.
